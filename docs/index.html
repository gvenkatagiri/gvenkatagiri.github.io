Why Book Reviews

Jan 12, 2023
While walking along the beaches of Pondicherry in Septmeber 2021, I was inspired while in conversation with my wife (a usual happenstance in my case & a testament to the gem of a person that she is) to mindfully make time for reading books. Since then I have managed to channel that inspiration on a regular basis and have been reading consistently. The joys of reading a book are continually reinforced for my with every new book that I read - it is perhaps the best way to expand one’s mind, accrue empathy & slowly add wisdom, one layer at a time.…
